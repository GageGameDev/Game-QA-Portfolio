# Bug #001 - Co-op Rating Reward Mismatch

**Game:** MLB The Show 26

**Platform:** PlayStation 5

**Category:** Progression / Rewards

**Severity:** High

**Status:** Open

**Date Reported:** July 10, 2026

---

## Summary

After completing a Ranked Co-op game, one player advanced to the next reward tier while the other player did not receive the same rating progression or rewards despite participating in the same match.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Start a Ranked Co-op game.
2. Complete the game.
3. View both players' post-game ratings.
4. Compare rating progression and rewards.

---

## Expected Result

Both players should receive the same rating progression and tier rewards based on the completed game.

---

## Actual Result

One player advanced to the next reward tier while the other player's rating and rewards did not update correctly.

---

## Frequency

Observed once.
