# Bug #002 - Defensive Player Partially Sinks into Field During Pre-Game Presentation

**Game:** MLB The Show 26

**Platform:** PlayStation 5

**Game Mode:** Ranked Co-op

**Category:** Visual / Animation

**Severity:** Low (Cosmetic)

**Priority:** Low

**Status:** Open

**Date Reported:** July 10, 2026

---

## Environment

**Game Version:** Latest version available (exact version unknown)

**Internet Connection:** Online

---

## Summary

During the pre-game presentation in Ranked Co-op, a defensive player positioned in front of the pitcher occasionally appears partially submerged beneath the playing field. The issue resolves itself before the first pitch is thrown and does not affect gameplay.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Start a Ranked Co-op game.
2. Allow the pre-game presentation to play.
3. Observe the defensive player positioned in front of the pitcher.
4. Continue watching until the first pitch.

---

## Expected Result

All defensive player models should remain fully above the playing surface throughout the pre-game presentation.

---

## Actual Result

One defensive player appears partially embedded in the field during the pre-game presentation. The player model returns to its correct position before the first pitch.

---

## Reproduction Rate

Observed more than **10 times** during Ranked Co-op games.

Estimated reproduction rate: **10/10 (100%)**

---

## Impact

- Cosmetic only.
- Does not affect gameplay.
- Resolves automatically before the first pitch.
- Reduces the visual polish of the pre-game presentation.
